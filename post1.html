Top 10 Anime of All Time

By 7AnimeVerse | August 1, 2025

1. Attack on Titan

Gripping, intense, and with one of the most complex plots in anime history. Titans, betrayal, freedom — this one has it all.

2. Death Note

A psychological battle between Light and L — brilliant writing, characters, and suspense in every episode.

3. Demon Slayer

Stunning visuals, heartfelt characters, and intense demon-hunting action. A modern favorite.

4. Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

A masterpiece of world-building, alchemy, brotherhood, and emotional depth. A must-watch for all anime fans.

5. One Piece

A never-ending adventure of friendship, dreams, and the sea. With 1000+ episodes, it’s the king of long-running anime.

6. Naruto Shippuden

The ultimate journey of an underdog becoming a legend. Packed with iconic battles and life lessons.

7. Hunter x Hunter

A smart shounen with deep themes, strong character development, and strategic action arcs.

8. My Hero Academia

Quirks, heroes, and emotional training arcs. A perfect entry for new anime fans.

9. Jujutsu Kaisen

Modern animation meets dark curses and sorcery. Clean fights and great character designs.

10. Code Geass

Strategic rebellion led by a masked genius. Twists, mechas, and one of the most memorable endings.
